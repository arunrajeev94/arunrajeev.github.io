Architecture

Architectural Design Thesis

Architectural Design Thesis undertaken as part of completion of the Bachelor in Architecture, 2016. The thesis project is an attempt to promote film and experimental music and encourage new thinking in the field of music and sound design by creating an architectural environment that resonates with the character of the art.

The site is located in Kochi,Kerala, a Southern state in India. Kochi is known historically for being a hub of spice trade and has been under the Dutch and British rule and this influence can be seen in many historical buildings within the city which was a starting point for the architecture of the project. The building program consists mainly of academic block, administrative block, housing and recreational facilities.

Urban Design

Architectural Design Studio 8, 2016.Architectural design studio 8 dealt with urban design. The primary aim of the design studio was to identify urban transects within the city of Mysore, located in the state of Karnataka, India. The intention was to identify site specific requirements and propose urban design interventions accordingly.

The master planning, zoning and building program development were undertaken as a group work and subsequently each member selected a part from the whole to be designed individually.The architecture of the building was kept minimal and functional taking cues from the local architectural style and opening up larger public plazas and gathering spaces.

Campus Design

Architectural Design Studio 7, 2015.The aim of architectural design studio 7 was to design an educational campus for three disciplines, Film, Photography and Fine Arts offering undergraduate degrees and diploma courses. The campus is meant to cater to 400-600 students.

The building program includes academic buildings for each institute, administration building,an auditorium, open-air theatre, library, cafe and art gallery and housing for students and faculty[master-plan only] The site is highly contoured and hence the design tried to utilise the natural contours by creating the building blocks in levels placing them along the contours and also creating streets in between them.
